Who We Are

A Specialist Lab at the Heart of Alzheimer Research

Our team of clinical neurophysiologists, neurologists, and neuroscientists is embedded in the Alzheimer Center Amsterdam at Amsterdam UMC — one of Europe's foremost dementia research institutions.

We specialise in resting-state EEG and MEG, functional network analysis, brain modelling, non-invasive stimulation, and treatment monitoring — with an unbroken focus on dementia for over two decades.

EEG & MEG Analysis

Capturing Brain Dynamics at Every Scale

EEG — Scalable & multi-site compatible

Captures millisecond-level neuronal dynamics at low cost. Clinically validated and deployable across sites in international trials.

MEG — High spatial resolution

Accesses deep structures including the hippocampus. Superior source localisation for mechanistic and biomarker studies.

Novel Connectivity & Network Measures

Proprietary functional connectivity markers developed and validated as diagnostic and treatment-monitoring biomarkers.
